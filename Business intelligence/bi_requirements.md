# BI Requirements - Hotel Reservation System

## **1. Business Goals**
- Optimize room pricing and occupancy
- Reduce booking cancellations
- Improve customer loyalty
- Support data-driven decisions

## **2. Key Users**
- **Hotel Manager**: Overall performance
- **Front Desk**: Daily operations
- **Revenue Manager**: Pricing strategy
- **Marketing**: Customer insights

## **3. Data Sources**
1. **Reservations** (main fact table)
2. **Customers** (dimension)
3. **Rooms** (dimension) 
4. **Payments** (financial)
5. **Holidays** (calendar)

## **4. Reports Needed**
| Report | Frequency | Audience |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Daily Occupancy | Daily | Operations |
| Weekly Revenue | Weekly | Managers |
| Monthly Performance | Monthly | Executives |
| Customer Analytics | Monthly | Marketing |

## **5. Technology Stack**
- **Database**: Oracle PL/SQL
- **ETL**: SQL scripts
- **Visualization**: Excel/Tableau (future)
- **Delivery**: PDF/Email dashboards
